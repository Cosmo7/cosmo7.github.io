Who we are

Bruce Dooley, CRS

bruce@dooleyco.com

614.297.8600

Bruce has lived in the Downtown/Short North area since 1979. He has seen (and been part of) the HUGE change in the area over the past 30+ years.

Before joining Keller Williams Classic Properties, Bruce owned Dooley & Company Realtors for 27 years. He is consistently ranked in the top 25 agents of CBR based on information from MLS. He has received the highest sales award given by CBR, the 25 Million Dollar award, & Realtor Broker of the Year for CBR in 1997.

In 2005, Bruce was inducted as a Charter Member of CBR, receiving the Lifetime Achievement Award.

With his sales skills, experience & education as well as hard work, ethics & honesty, he will do a great job for you in buying and/or selling your home.
